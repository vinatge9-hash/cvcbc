Our Story

Cup & Bean started as a small neighborhood café with a big dream: to bring people together over great coffee and warm, honest pastries. We source ethically grown beans, support local bakers, and cultivate a welcoming space where everyone feels at home.

Whether you're meeting a friend, catching up on work, or simply taking a quiet moment for yourself, we’ve crafted a simple, friendly experience with attention to detail and care in every cup.
